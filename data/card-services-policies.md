# Card Services Policies (Mock)

## Below are the Card Services Policies for Alec's Bank of Ohio

## Name of Bank: Alec's Bank of Ohio

## Card Activation
- New cards must be activated before use.
- Activation can be completed via:
  - Mobile banking app
  - Automated phone system
  - Customer service representative
- Activation requires verification of the customer’s identity.

## Declined Transactions
Common reasons for declined transactions include:
- Insufficient available balance
- Exceeded daily spending limits
- Merchant category restrictions
- Suspected fraud (see Fraud Policies)

Customer service may:
- Explain the decline reason if visible
- Confirm available balance and limits
- Escalate to Fraud if suspicious activity is detected

## Card Replacement
Replacement cards may be issued for:
- Lost cards
- Stolen cards
- Damaged or non‑functional cards
- Compromised card numbers

# Card Replacement Delivery

## Expedited Delivery
Replacement cards sent with expedited delivery arrive within **1–2 business days**.

## Standard Delivery
Replacement cards sent with standard delivery arrive within **5–7 business days**.

## PIN Resets
- PIN resets require identity verification.
- PINs cannot be shared verbally.
- Customers may reset PINs via approved secure channels.

## Card Limits
- Daily purchase and ATM withdrawal limits are predefined.
- Temporary limit increases may be requested.
- Approval is subject to account standing and internal policy.

## Out of Scope
- Fraud investigations
- Refunds or chargebacks
- Merchant dispute resolution
