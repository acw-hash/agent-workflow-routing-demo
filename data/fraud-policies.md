# Fraud Policies (Mock)

## What Is Considered Fraud
Fraud includes:
- Unauthorized card transactions
- Account takeover attempts
- Identity theft
- Suspicious activity not initiated by the customer

## Reporting Fraud
Customers should report fraud immediately if:
- They do not recognize a transaction
- They suspect their card or account is compromised
- They receive suspicious alerts or messages

## Fraud Investigation Process
- Transactions are reviewed by the fraud team.
- Temporary card restrictions may be applied.
- A replacement card may be issued if compromise is confirmed.
- Investigations typically take several business days.

## Customer Protections
- Customers are not held liable for confirmed unauthorized transactions.
- Provisional credits may be issued during investigation (not guaranteed).
- Final resolution depends on investigation outcome.

## What Customer Service Can Do
- Document reported fraud concerns
- Explain next steps in the investigation
- Route the case to the fraud review team

## What Customer Service Cannot Do
- Guarantee outcomes or credits
- Provide legal advice
- Reverse transactions immediately

## Out of Scope
- Refund status inquiries
- Chargeback processing
- General card servicing questions unrelated to security
