# Refunds and Disputes Policies (Mock)

## Refund Types
Refunds may occur due to:
- Merchant-initiated refunds
- Billing errors
- Chargebacks approved after dispute review

## Merchant Refunds
- Refund timing depends on the merchant.
- Typical processing time: 3–10 business days.
- Banks do not control merchant refund speed.

## Transaction Disputes
Customers may dispute transactions if:
- They were charged incorrectly
- They did not receive goods or services
- The transaction amount is incorrect

## Chargeback Process
- Dispute is submitted with required details.
- Merchant is contacted for review.
- Temporary credit may be issued during review.
- Final decision is based on documentation.

## Required Information
- Transaction date and amount
- Merchant name
- Reason for dispute
- Supporting documentation (if available)

## What Refund Agents Can Do
- Explain refund or dispute status
- Outline next steps and timelines
- Confirm whether documentation is pending

## What Refund Agents Cannot Do
- Expedite merchant refunds
- Override dispute outcomes
- Investigate fraud (see Fraud Policies)

## Out of Scope
- Unauthorized transaction investigations
- Card activation or replacement
- Account security issues
